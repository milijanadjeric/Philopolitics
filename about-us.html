philopolitics

is an independent research center established to
explore political, social and cultural alternatives
and models that would contribute to greater civic
participation, solidarity and dignity of individuals
and groups in order to create peaceful, just
and inclusive societies.

The Goals of the Association are:

Improvement of knowledge and skills of social, political
and cultural activism;

Education of citizens, especially young people about the
importance and possibilities of social, political and cultural
activism, organization of activities, cooperation and
networking of institutions, organizations and individuals
engaged in social, political cultural activism and
research.
